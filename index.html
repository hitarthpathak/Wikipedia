Hitarth Pathak, known mononymously as HITARTH, is an independent artist from Rajasthan, India. He is an Actor, Singer, YouTuber, Blogger and a Computer Programmer.

Early Life & Background

Hitarth was born on 17 August 2001 in a Hindu Brahmin family in Banswara in Rajasthan, India. His parents are Deepak Pathak and Varsha Pathak. He has a younger brother named Mitarth Pathak.

Education

Hitarth completed his school education from three different schools in Banswara. He completed his kindergarten from Maa Bharti School. Then he moved to St. Thomas School and after that, he moved to Tiny Tots Senior Secondary School, where he completed his secondary and senior secondary education. He also completed RS-CIT course during the time of his school. In 2019, he moved to Jaipur for his college studies. He attended St. Wilfred's PG College and pursued Bachelor of Science in Biology. In November 2023, Hitarth started learning full stack web development and proceed his career in that field, exploring his artistic work side by side.

Personal Life

Hitarth believes in Hinduism and is politically moderate. He is very self-conscious and takes good care of his health and hygiene. He likes travelling, playing badminton, and is very foodie. He is a teetotaller and is socially ambivert. He is currently single.

Creative Works

Hitarth was a Scout in his school days. He also enrolled for NSS volunteership in college. He was very active in extracurricular activities in school and college days, including singing, dancing, drama, speech and anchoring[1][2][3]. Apart from this, he qualified so many competitive exams like Hindi Rashtrabhasha, NTSE, Olympiads (NSO and IMO), etc. in school. He has a bunch of certificates and medals. He also participated in a district-level Table Tennis tournament.

World Of Hitarth

Hitarth created his YouTube channel on 10 August 2020 with the name "Hit-Art", later he renamed it to "HITARTH". There he started making educational videos in slide-by-slide format. Then he started uploading rare, historical and educational videos from his own collection, along with his own created videos in between. However, he could not upload most of the content due to copyright issues.

He created his Blogging site somewhere in mid 2020, where he started writing about historical and mysterious content.

In July 2022, he made his first short film "Who's There" along with his brothers.

On 5 November 2022, he merged all of his work and created "World Of Hitarth", a platform for his all artistic works. He created a biolink platform and added links of all of his work and has been using that as his own website. Since then, all his work comes under the banner of "Hit-Art Productions".
